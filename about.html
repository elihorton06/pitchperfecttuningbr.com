About Eli

He who has ears, let him tune!

Hey everyone! I'm Eli — a Certified Piano Technician (CPT) through the Piano Technicians Academy, and a lifelong musician trained in classical piano since 2011. Music has always been a big part of my life, and now I'm blessed to use that passion to help others enjoy their instruments at home. From a young age, I believe God blessed me with perfect pitch, and that gift has shaped the way I hear, play, and work with music.

Born and raised in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, I started Pitch Perfect Piano Tuning & Repair with a heart to serve people. It began as a way to help out family and friends, but word spread quickly — and before long, the Lord began blessing me with more opportunities and connections than I could’ve imagined. I believe God gave me both the hands and the heart to work, train, and build relationships through music.

Customer care is always my top priority — I aim to make your piano sound its absolute best and leave your home knowing I've done my best work. Thanks for supporting local business, and I hope to serve you and your family for years to come!
